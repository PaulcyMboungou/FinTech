MoMo Congo

Mobile Money in Republic of Congo refers to financial services provided through mobile phones, enabling users to perform transactions without needing a traditional bank account. It plays a crucial role in financial inclusion, given the country's vast size and limited banking infrastructure.

Merchant Payments

Enables businesses to accept payments from customers through mobile money.

Savings and Loans

Some providers offer savings accounts and microloans.

Bill Payments

Utility bills, school fees, and other services can be paid via mobile money.

Money Transfers

Users can send and receive money domestically and internationally.
